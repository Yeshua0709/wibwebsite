FAQs

How to Order?

- Before ordering please be sure that you’re 100% sure when placing an order.
- Send us a direct message through our Facebook Page.
- Fill out the necessary information with your order
- After submitting, please wait for your confirmation email to be sent estimated within 24-72 hours.
- Once confirmation e-mail is sent, you may now pay within the DEADLINE and TIME allotted. Send your POP’s (Proof of Payments) on another form provided on the confirmation e-mail sent.
- Wait for your Tracking Number to be sent via e-mail/direct message!
